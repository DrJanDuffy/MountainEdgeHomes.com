Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026. This policy describes how Mountain Edge Homes (“we,” “us”) handles information when you use mountainedgehomes.com and related contact channels.

Who we are

Mountain Edge Homes provides real estate services in Mountain’s Edge and southwest Las Vegas, Nevada. Office: 8015 Blue Diamond Rd, Las Vegas, NV 89178. Phone: (702) 919-5002. Email: DrJanSells@MountainEdgeHomes.com.

Information we collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as name, email address, phone number, and property or real estate interests when you:

  • Call, email, or text us
  • Schedule an appointment through our online scheduling tools (including embedded Calendly)
  • Use other interactive features on this site

We also receive standard technical data from your browser (e.g., general location derived from IP, device type, and pages viewed) through cookies and similar technologies where applicable.

How we use information

We use this information to respond to inquiries, provide real estate services, improve our website, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or analytics tools (for example, Google Analytics when configured) to understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

When you use our embedded scheduling experience, Calendly may set cookies or similar technologies on its domain as part of booking. We may configure embeds to reduce redundant notices where appropriate; you should still ensure your overall site disclosures meet applicable laws (such as GDPR or CCPA) if you serve visitors in those jurisdictions.

Third-party services

Our site may embed or link to third-party tools (such as Calendly for scheduling, property search widgets, or maps). Those providers have their own privacy policies and handle data according to their terms. We encourage you to review their policies when you use those features.

Your choices

You may contact us to opt out of non-essential communications or to request access or correction of your information where applicable. Nevada residents may have additional rights under applicable state law; contact us using the information above.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest revision.

Contact
